Urgency Guidelines

Please submit blood requests only for **genuine medical requirements**. Once submitted, matching donors in our city will receive an alert.

Critical: Needs matching within 2 hours. Urgent: Required within 12-24 hours. Normal: Planned surgeries or routine requirements.
